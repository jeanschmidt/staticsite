About R&B

Founded in September/2013, with resources from prestigious financial and strategic investors, R&B has became a reference at tracking and tracing medicinal products and data custody.

Products

R&B has developed 3 main products for pharmaceutical sector:

  • R&BTRACK,
  • DATAFARMAR&B
  • R&BCONSUMER

That allow all the elements of the supply chain to fulfil the ANVISA's RD54 standards.

Our Goal

To become the main business of traceability of the country, attending the principal sectors with excellence.
